Descriptions

On November 16th, 2022, Femina Hip, with the Danish and Swedish Embassies, celebrated 20 years of Fema Clubs. These extracurricular programs promote youth leadership, teamwork, and life skills in Tanzanian schools, with over 2,370 clubs, 230,000 members, and 81 networks. Fema Clubs have impacted 15 million young people over two decades, with support from mentors and key government and civil society partners. The event celebrated this milestone and the contributions of key partners in government, civil society, and corporate institutions.

Our role encompassed managing event logistics and overseeing registration, ensuring the presence of trained registration staff and ushers to facilitate a smooth and professional experience.
