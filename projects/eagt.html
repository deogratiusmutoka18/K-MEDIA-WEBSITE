Descriptions

"Got Talent" is a global brand that has held several shows around the world, including the famous "America's Got Talent' and "Britain's Got Talent". In 2019 the show was brought to East Africa. The project was franchised by Clouds Media Group International, one of East & Central Africa's most respected media houses, and produced by Rapid Blue, one of the leading production companies in Africa, which made the South African and Nigerian rendering of the show. EAGT was launched in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda; the launch in Dar-es-salaam was attended by the Minister of Information, Sports and Culture Dr. Harrison Mwakyembe.

Our

Role

01

Our role was to be a part of the local production crew of the show. We worked with a production team from Kenya, South African producers, and Clouds Media Group in producing the show. This included scouting for talent, coordinating auditions, being part of the production crew, as well as hiring and training of the local production staff. Before shooting the show, we reached out to diverse communities within Tanzania; scouting for talent and sending video auditions of participants for the first and second rounds of auditions. On the day of production, we registered final participants for auditions and helped facilitate the entire production process.
